About my 2nd Mural

This mural was designed for another school that had recently done construction over-top of a previously done mural. This was my first mural ever done that was not made from scratch and kept the previous artist theme!

Inspiration

The inspiration behind this mural was mainly to keep the previous artist Ideas while changing the design to fit around the newly constructed door.

Planning

This mural was painted along a much longer wall than my first mural, and had multiple design aspects that needed to be pieced together correctly to make sense with the broader picture. My planning process was very in depth with creating these designs while tying them tgehter. I also added my own piece with a balloon cart to subtract away from the dead space up front.

Experience

This mural took a longer time to complete. I worked on this mural alone besides the company of friends who would regularly watch the work unfold. The best part was the joy of the children returning back to their classrooms to find the transformation, and the approval of the parents and teachers.
